## Notes for Revision

**Current Tone Issues:**
- Heavy anti-VC framing ("rigged game," "fighting back," "not your friends")
- Adversarial positioning may alienate potential VC partners
- Could feel paranoid rather than empowering

**Goals for Revision:**
- Keep the founder-first energy and empowerment
- Soften combative language toward VCs
- Focus on efficiency and smart matching vs. "fighting"
- Maintain authenticity without alienating either side of the marketplace
