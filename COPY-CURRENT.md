## The Problem We See

**Section Title:** The Problem We See

**Body:**
The investment landscape is complex. Founders often spend more time pitching than building. Let's change that.

Every pitch to a wrong-fit investor is time away from ==building the thing that matters==.

**Stats:**
- 100:1 — Information gathered vs. investments made by VCs
- 200 — Hours typically spent in a failed fundraising cycle
- 2% — Success rate (without precise targeting)

**Blockquote:** "A good deck and lots of meetings aren't the direct path to investment. ==It's about the right match.=="

**Section Title:** Why We Built This
